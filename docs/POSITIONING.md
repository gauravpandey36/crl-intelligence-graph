# CRL Intelligence Graph — positioning

**One line:** They tell you what happened to the industry. We show you what it means for YOUR pipeline and YOUR CDMOs — as a graph, CRLs included, fed by your own data. Free and open (MIT).

**The question incumbents can't answer:** "Is this MY risk?" Redica/FDAzilla (merged), AgencyIQ, Cortellis are paywalled, document/search-centric, not graph-native, and do not fuse Complete Response Letters or let you cross-reference your own vendors/programs.

**Our four differentiators (honest):**
1. CRLs as first-class graph nodes, linked to the same facility/sponsor as later Warning Letters/483s.
2. Graph/network views — facility→CDMO→deficiency→cascade traversals a search box can't do.
3. "Is this MY risk?" — upload your vendor/CDMO list, get flagged against FDA watch lists + an exposure index.
4. Open + free + auditable, no paywall, no per-seat tax.

**Where incumbents genuinely beat us (do NOT overclaim):** Redica's EIR depth (FOIA), 30K+ cleaned inspector profiles, multi-agency coverage, enterprise support; AgencyIQ/Cortellis forward-looking policy intel. We win on STRUCTURE + CRL inclusion + your-data fusion + free/open, not on raw data volume.

**Every public surface carries:** "Educational, built on public FDA records. Not regulatory advice; discuss with qualified counsel." Free, MIT, no monetization.
